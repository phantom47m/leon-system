# Social Media Scheduler

Plan and draft your entire social media calendar with AI. Stop staring at blank screens — get platform-optimized content ready to post.

## What It Does

- **Content calendars** — weekly or monthly, organized by platform and content type
- **Platform-optimized drafts** — tailored copy for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook
- **Content pillars** — define your themes and rotate consistently
- **Hashtag strategy** — tiered hashtags for reach and discovery
- **Repurposing maps** — turn one idea into posts across every platform

## Example Usage

> "Create a 2-week content calendar for my SaaS startup"

> "Write a LinkedIn post about our new feature launch"

> "Turn this blog post into a Twitter thread and Instagram carousel"

> "What should I post this week? My pillars are: education, behind-the-scenes, customer stories"

## Who It's For

Solo founders, marketing teams, content creators, agencies — anyone who needs consistent social media without the overwhelm.
